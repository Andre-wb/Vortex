    Stake & register fee

    Stake0 SOL
    Register fee paid—

    The register fee (1 SOL, non-refundable) is paid once per node to anchor identity and discourage sybils. Stake is optional — it multiplies your share of the rewards pool.

    How rewards are calculated

    1. 70% of platform premium revenue goes into the rewards pool.
    2. Your share = 0.7 × (users_on_your_node / total_premium_users) + 0.3 × √(stake / total_stake).
    3. Uptime acts as a multiplier — full credit at 99%+, zeroed below 50%.
    4. Payout is automatic once the smart contract is deployed; amounts below are estimates.
