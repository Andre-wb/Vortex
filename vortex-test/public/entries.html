Entry URLs

Starting points for a client that has no peers yet. Each entry URL is a way to reach the controller — through a Cloudflare tunnel, Tor, IPFS gateway, or a direct domain. Copy any one into your Vortex app on first launch.

TUNNEL Cloudflare tunnel

Free *.trycloudflare.com address. No account required. URL changes when the tunnel restarts — treat as ephemeral.

TOR Hidden service

.onion address, reachable only through Tor. Censor-proof, slower. Needs a Tor client or bridge.

IPFS Distributed content

Content-addressed copy of the static frontend. Served from any IPFS gateway — no single server to block.

DIRECT Plain HTTPS

Regular domain + TLS. Fastest but blockable at the DNS/IP level.
